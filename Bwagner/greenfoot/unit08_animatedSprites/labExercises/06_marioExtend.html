Mario Extend

Download and Open Scenario

  1. Click on the following link to download the scenario: Mario Extend

  2. Select Open.

  3. Copy the folder Mario Extend and paste it in your myGreenfoot folder.

  4. Launch Greenfoot and select Open from the Scenario menu.

  5. Navigate to your MyGreenfoot folder, select the Mario Extend scenario, and click Open.


Add a new feature to the Mario scenario. Here are a few examples, but you don't have to limit yourself to these. Be creative.

  • Give Mario a new ability (shoot laser, do flips, float in a bubble, become a giant).

  • Add a new obstacle that Mario must avoid. (insect, animal, alien, bullet, paint ball, quicksand).

  • Create a point system. (Power-ups, scoreboard, hit points).

  • Create another world for Mario to explore.

Fill free to remove or modify any of the features already included.
